FAQ'S

Q: What is AEC Library?

This is an Open-Source Library Website in which you get Resources to learn different topics, Donate book section to donate your old books, and a Book issue section to keep a record of all the books issued.

Q: What is the best part of AEC Library?

The best part about the website is that you can also contribute to the website for others to use the resourses by donating the books you have , all categories of books are welcomed for donation.

Q: How to become a member?

By donating any book of your choice you can become a member of the AEC Library.

Q: What is the registration fee?

There is no registration fees! One simply needs to donate a book to become a member.

Q: I forgot my login credentials. What should I do?

No worries! Click on the "Forgot your Password?" link. An email containing the instructions to reset your password will be sent to your registered email-ID

Q: I can't find the book I am looking for. What to do?

The library has a huge collection of books of multiple genres. However, if you can't find the book you're looking for, do reach out to us! Drop us a message through the "Contact Us" section.

Q: How can I donate a book?

Click on the "Donate books" button and fill out the form with your details. We will reach out to you with further details.

Q: What is the benefits of an account in Aec-Library ?

  1. Contriibute to Aec-library : Aec-library is a community initiative to build a robust book catalog.Anyone can add or edit data about books and blogs.
  2. Personalize your experience : We are always adding new features to our site so you can make your experience more personal .

Q: How can I find a book here?

Go to the "CHoose and read books" section and search the book you are looking for .

Q: I haven't found a book but it's not readable format>Is something wrong?

This is not supposed to happen but if it happens please leave a message on out "Contact us" section.We will definitely look in it.

Q: How can I download a book?

Go to our "Recommended Book" secion. Choose your book which you want to download then click on the "Download as pdf". Happy reading :)

Q: How many books can I check-out at once?

You can check-out as many books as you want.

Q: How can I access in Online library?

Go to our "Resources" section.Cick on the "All resources". You can find out in our environment all recources to satisfy your need.
