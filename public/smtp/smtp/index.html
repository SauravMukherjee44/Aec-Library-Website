What we do

With the state-of-art digital platform, we integrate a digital healthcare ecosystem with major stakeholders like healthcare consumers, doctors, chemists, hospitals, blood banks, ambulance services.

As a part of integrated healthcare solutions, we also connect with US FDA / CE Certified smart diagnostic devices. Health ATM, CHT Health Monitor, CHT Urine Analyser, CHT ECG Monitor, CHT Vitastiq, CHT Fetal Doppler are few smart digital diagnostics connected with HealthNovo Platform.

Our Solutions

PHC refers to ‘Essential health care’, based on scientifically tested and socially acceptable methods & technology. Its purpose is to make universal health care accessible to all individuals and families in a community. With the help of PHCs, we will offer below services –

Mapping Consumers

Reaching out to people digitally who require healthcare solutions

Doctor Consultation

Providing consultation for patients in person or arranging remote video consultation

Medicine Dispensing

Ensuring availability of medicines at the centre, local pharmacies and online also

Medical Camps

Conducting medical camps for health checkups and consultations

Conducting
Diagnostic Tests

Diagnosis plays a vital role in detecting the type of disease. We perform diagnostic tests, offering Point of care testing services and second screening to name a few

Health Wallet Data Storage and Alerts follow-ups

Ensuring that the medical history of patients is recorded and time-to-time follow-ups are sent to them

Blood Bank /
Ambulance Services

Our point of care devices

Health ATM

A compact machine designed to diagnose chronic diseases related to cardiology, neurology, pulmonary testing, gynaecology, basic laboratory testing and emergency facilities. It provides an instant output and goes easy on the pocket.

CHT Health Monitor

A multi-functional health monitoring device configured with a sensor based facility used for measuring blood pressure along with basic vitals such as heart rate, blood oxygen & respiration, body temperature, blood glucose and one lead ECG.

*Above devices are supported by strong channel partners who assure that products are USA FDA/ CE Certified and the process of manufacturing is ISO certified.
